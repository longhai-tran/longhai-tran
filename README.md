## 👨‍💻 About Me

> *"The quality of a verification engineer is measured not by the bugs they find, but by the bugs they prevent."*

I am a **self-driven engineer** on a structured journey from zero to a professional **Design Verification Engineer**. My focus is on building deep, industry-relevant expertise in **functional verification** of digital hardware — from writing clean RTL in Verilog to building advanced verification environments in SystemVerilog/UVM.

```
📍 Location    : Vietnam
🎯 Target Role : Design Verification Engineer (DV/DVE)
📅 Timeline    : Actively building my portfolio through 2025-2026
📬 Email       : longhai.engineer@gmail.com
🔗 LinkedIn    : linkedin.com/in/long-hải-trần-19b505400/
```

**What drives me:** The intellectual challenge of finding the corner cases that escape everyone else — that one obscure protocol violation or timing edge case hiding in a million-line design. Verification is where engineering meets detective work.
